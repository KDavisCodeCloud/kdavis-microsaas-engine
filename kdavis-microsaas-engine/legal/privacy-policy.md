## 1. What We Collect

### Information you provide
- Account registration data: name, email address, company name

### Information collected automatically
- Usage events: feature interactions, session length, workflow executions
- Technical data: IP address (country level only), browser type, device type
- Cookies: session authentication only — no advertising or tracking cookies

### Information from third parties
- Stripe: subscription status, payment events
- Supabase Auth: authentication tokens

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

- Provide, operate, and improve the Service
- Process payments and manage your subscription
- Send transactional emails: receipts, usage digests, re-engagement (behavioral, not marketing)
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data to train AI models without explicit consent.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Isolation

- Your data is stored in an isolated, per-tenant database partition
- Row-level security enforced at the database level — no cross-tenant data access
- Data is encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Your data never leaves your logical tenant environment during processing

---

## 4. Data Retention

- Active account data: retained for the duration of your subscription
- After cancellation: data retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted on request
- Backup retention: 30 days maximum
- Usage logs: 12 months, then aggregated and anonymized
